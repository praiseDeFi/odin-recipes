Description

This homemade pizza recipe delivers a crispy crust topped with rich tomato sauce, gooey mozzarella cheese, and your favorite toppings. Perfect for family dinners or weekend treats, it’s easy to make and customizable to suit every taste.

Ingredientsa

  • 2 ½ cups all-purpose flour
  • 1 tsp salt
  • 1 tsp sugar
  • 1 tbsp olive oil
  • 1 packet (2 ¼ tsp) active dry yeast
  • 1 cup warm water
  • ½ cup pizza sauce
  • 1 ½ cups shredded mozzarella cheese

Stepsa

  1. In a small bowl, mix warm water and yeast. Let it sit for 5–10 minutes until foamy.
  2. In a large bowl, combine flour, salt, and sugar. Add the yeast mixture and olive oil.
  3. Mix and knead the dough for 5–7 minutes until smooth.
  4. Cover the dough and let it rise for 1 hour, or until doubled in size.
  5. Preheat your oven to 475°F (245°C).
  6. Roll out the dough on a floured surface to your desired thickness.
  7. Transfer the dough to a baking tray or pizza stone.
  8. Spread pizza sauce evenly over the dough.
  9. Add mozzarella cheese and your desired toppings.
  10. Bake for 12–15 minutes or until the crust is golden and cheese is bubbly.
  11. Remove from the oven, let it cool slightly, slice, and enjoy!
